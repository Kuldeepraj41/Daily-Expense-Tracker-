🧠 How RAG Works
1. Retrieve
Your query is vectorised using TF-IDF. Cosine similarity finds the most relevant expense records and financial knowledge.
2. Augment
Retrieved documents are combined with your question to provide grounded context.
3. Generate
An intent-aware NLG engine crafts a personalised, data-driven answer.
